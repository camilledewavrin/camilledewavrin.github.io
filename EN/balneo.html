« Want to laze around ? Come and enjoy our balneotherapy space. »

Our private balneotherapy space will take you out of time, through bubbles in the Teuco Bathtub with essential oils from the brand « Camylle Spa Aromatherapy ».

All options include preparation to care (shower and body scrub) and a bubble care in the Teuco Bathtub (to be chosen in a specific list). You can also go for a massage (different in each option).

NOTHING BUT BUBBLES ! (45min)

This option includes preparation and one bubble care.

FOND OF BUBBLES (60min)

This option includes preparation, one bubble care and a 15min-massage (head or feet or hands).

ESSENTIAL BUBBLES (75min)

This option includes preparation, one bubble care and a 30min-massage, to be determined.

*Our cares are non therapeutical and non medicalized.
